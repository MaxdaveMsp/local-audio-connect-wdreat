Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is StreamConnect?
A: StreamConnect is an iOS app that helps you connect to local audio streams on your network. It's perfect for home audio servers, local broadcasts, and network audio systems.
Q: How do I connect to an audio stream?
A: You have two options:
  1. Manual Entry: Enter the IP address and port number on the home screen, then tap "Connect"
  2. QR Code: Tap "Scan QR Code" and point your camera at a QR code containing the stream address
Q: What format should the IP address be in?
A: Enter the IP address in standard format (e.g., 192.168.1.100) and the port number separately (e.g., 8080). The app will combine them into the full URL.
Q: How do I save a stream for later?
A: When connected to a stream, tap the bookmark icon in the top-right corner. Enter a custom name for the stream and tap "Save". You can access saved streams from the "Saved Streams" tab.
Q: Can I listen with my screen locked?
A: Yes! StreamConnect supports background audio playback. Once connected to a stream, you can lock your screen or switch to other apps, and the audio will continue playing.
Q: Do I need an internet connection?
A: No, you only need to be on the same local network as the audio stream. StreamConnect works great on local networks without internet access.
Q: Is my data private?
A: Absolutely! StreamConnect stores all data locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your information on external servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Q: What audio formats are supported?
A: StreamConnect uses a WebView to access your stream's web interface, so it supports whatever audio formats your stream server provides (typically MP3, AAC, or other web-compatible formats).
Q: Why can't I connect to my stream?
A: Check these common issues:
  • Ensure you're on the same network as the audio stream
  • Verify the IP address and port are correct
  • Make sure the audio server is running
  • Check if your network has firewall restrictions
  • Try accessing the stream URL in Safari first to verify it works
Q: How do I edit or delete saved streams?
A: Go to the "Saved Streams" tab. Tap the pencil icon to edit a stream's name, or tap the trash icon to delete it. You can also swipe left on any stream to reveal delete option.
Q: Can I use StreamConnect with Bluetooth headphones?
A: Yes! StreamConnect works with all audio output devices supported by iOS, including Bluetooth headphones, AirPods, and external speakers.
Q: Is StreamConnect free?
A: Yes, StreamConnect is completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions.

Troubleshooting

Connection Issues

Problem: Can't connect to stream

Solutions:

  1. Verify you're on the same WiFi network as the stream
  2. Check the IP address and port are correct
  3. Test the URL in Safari: http://[IP]:[PORT]
  4. Restart your router and audio server
  5. Check firewall settings on your network

QR Code Scanner Issues

Problem: QR scanner not working

Solutions:

  1. Grant camera permission in iOS Settings ‚Üí StreamConnect
  2. Ensure good lighting when scanning
  3. Hold camera steady and at proper distance
  4. Make sure QR code contains valid URL format

Audio Playback Issues

Problem: No sound or audio cuts out

Solutions:

  1. Check device volume and mute switch
  2. Verify audio is playing on the stream server
  3. Check network connection stability
  4. Try disconnecting and reconnecting
  5. Restart the app

Background Playback Issues

Problem: Audio stops when screen locks

Solutions:

  1. Ensure Low Power Mode is disabled
  2. Check Background App Refresh is enabled
  3. Verify the stream supports background audio
  4. Try restarting the app

Tips & Tricks

Creating QR Codes

To create a QR code for your audio stream:

  1. Use any QR code generator (e.g., qr-code-generator.com)
  2. Enter your stream URL: http://[IP]:[PORT]
  3. Generate and download the QR code
  4. Share with others or print for easy access

Organizing Saved Streams

Use descriptive names for saved streams:

  • "Living Room Audio" instead of "Stream 1"
  • "Office Radio" instead of "192.168.1.100"
  • "Garage Speakers" instead of "Audio Stream"

Network Setup

For best results:

  • Use a stable WiFi connection
  • Keep your device close to the router
  • Avoid network congestion during streaming
  • Consider using 5GHz WiFi for better performance
