StreamConnect

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

TL;DR: StreamConnect does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data stays on your device. We respect your privacy completely.

Introduction

StreamConnect ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect any personal data.

StreamConnect does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your usage
  • Store data on external servers
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Display advertisements

Local Data Storage

StreamConnect stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Saved Stream URLs: The IP addresses and ports you save for quick access
  • Stream Names: Custom names you assign to saved streams
  • Creation Dates: When you saved each stream

This data is stored using AsyncStorage, a local storage system on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

Permissions

StreamConnect requests the following permissions:

Camera Permission

Purpose: To scan QR codes containing audio stream addresses.

Usage: The camera is only used when you explicitly tap the "Scan QR Code" button. No photos or videos are captured, stored, or transmitted. The camera only reads QR code data.

Network Access

Purpose: To connect to audio streams on your local network.

Usage: Network access is used only to load the audio stream URLs you provide. We do not monitor, log, or transmit your network activity.

Background Audio

StreamConnect uses background audio capabilities to continue playing audio when your screen is locked or when you switch to another app. This feature does not collect or transmit any data.

Third-Party Services

StreamConnect does NOT integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services
  • Crash reporting services

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using a strong device passcode
  • Keeping your iOS updated
  • Not jailbreaking your device

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Individual Streams: Swipe left on any saved stream and tap delete
  • All Data: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data

Since no data is stored on external servers, uninstalling the app completely removes all your data.

Children's Privacy

StreamConnect does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or App Store update notes.

International Users

StreamConnect can be used anywhere in the world. Since we don't collect or transmit any data, there are no international data transfer concerns.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no data for us to:

  • Access or provide to you
  • Correct or update
  • Delete from our servers
  • Transfer to another service

You have complete control over your data as it exists only on your device.

Legal Basis

We process the minimal local data (saved streams) based on your consent and for the legitimate purpose of providing the app's functionality. You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting saved streams or uninstalling the app.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information. However, since StreamConnect does not collect any personal information, these rights do not apply as there is no data to access, delete, or opt-out from.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), European users have specific rights regarding their personal data. However, since StreamConnect does not collect or process any personal data, these rights do not apply as there is no data to access, rectify, erase, or port.
