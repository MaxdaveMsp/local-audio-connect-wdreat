## FAQ

**Q: What is StreamConnect?**
A: StreamConnect is an iOS app that helps you connect to local audio streams on your network.

**Q: Do I need an account?**
A: No, StreamConnect requires no account or registration.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes, all data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or transmit any personal information.

**Q: What audio formats are supported?**
A: StreamConnect uses a WebView to access your stream's web interface, so it supports whatever your stream server provides.

**Q: Can I listen with the screen locked?**
A: Yes, StreamConnect supports background audio playback.

**Q: How do I save a stream?**
A: Connect to a stream, then tap the bookmark icon to save it with a custom name.

**Q: Does it work without internet?**
A: Yes, as long as you're on the same local network as the audio stream.

**Q: Is it free?**
A: Yes, StreamConnect is completely free with no ads or in-app purchases.
