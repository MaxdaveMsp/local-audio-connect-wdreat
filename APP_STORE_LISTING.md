## Privacy Policy (Summary)

**StreamConnect Privacy Policy**

Last Updated: January 2025

**Data Collection:**
StreamConnect does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

**Local Storage:**
- Saved stream URLs and names are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
- No data is synced to cloud services
- No account or login required

**Permissions:**
- **Camera:** Used only for QR code scanning. No photos or videos are stored.
- **Network:** Required to connect to audio streams on your local network.

**Third-Party Services:**
StreamConnect does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

**Data Deletion:**
All data can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing individual saved streams within the app.

**Contact:**
For privacy questions, contact: privacy@streamconnect.app

## App Review Notes

**Testing Instructions:**

To test the app, you can use any local HTTP server with an audio stream. For testing purposes:

1. **Manual Connection Test:**
   - Enter any valid local IP address and port
   - Example: 192.168.1.100:8080
   - The app will attempt to load the URL in a WebView

2. **QR Code Test:**
   - Generate a QR code containing a URL like "http://192.168.1.100:8080"
   - Scan with the app's QR scanner
   - The app will navigate to the WebView with that URL

3. **Save Stream Test:**
   - Connect to any stream
   - Tap the bookmark icon
   - Enter a name and save
   - Navigate to "Saved Streams" tab to verify

4. **Background Audio:**
   - Connect to a stream with audio
   - Lock the device
   - Audio should continue playing

**Special Configuration:** None required
